About IM Series Plate

The IM series number plate ranging from 1 to 9999 is a special series plate approved by Jabatan Pengangkutan Jalan for Persatuan Bola Sepak Negeri Sarawak.


The sale of special plate is undertaken by NFORT MSC SDN BHD a subsidiary company of FAS Holding and the holding company for Persatuan Bola Sepak Negeri Sarawak
